Our Humble Beginnings

Baking started as a sort of stress-relieving activity to me, Eloise(owner), while I was a homebased medical transcriptionist back in 2010 to 2016. It was also a way of bonding with my daughter, who was a toddler at that time. What a joy to see her much into whatever I baked! I also find time to bake simple treats whenever we have occasions at home where I get compliments from visitors.

In December 2016, two of my close friends asked me to make their wedding cake which I was too hesitant because I just know the basics. I know I lacked skills especially in decorating cakes. Too bad they are persistent and even sent their budget into my bank account, that way I cannot say NO anymore. I was afraid it might be damaged while on transport that is why I suggested using fondant even if I have zero knowledge handling this thing. Days after, I watched a lot of YouTube tutorials and then practiced on a smaller cake. When that beautiful day came. I had no sleep and no time to take a bath (oops. I’m one of the bridesmaid pa but the cake is more important, any bakers could relate to this…haha!), my husband and brother assisted in carrying the cake to the venue. I then assembled a 3-tier cake in the venue with the help of my friends. This is the cake attached in this Story. Seeing my newlywed friends and guests happy with the outcome made all the past efforts so worth it!

Life continues until March of the next year when someone contacted me to make yet another wedding cake for her cousin to be travelled to Sablan, Benguet as their cake decorator backed out! I wanted to alleviate their stress so I took the challenge again. After receiving positive feedbacks and the couples were grateful, I thought “well, maybe I can take this seriously.” So I posted these 2 wedding cakes and took it from there.

No words can describe how thankful I am to these 3 important people who made me realized where my PASSION is and that is to express myself through home-baked treats and ensure that customers will be satisfied.

With customized cakes that we do, we are honored to be part of our client’s celebrations… Check out our pinned menu for other treats that we offer too!
